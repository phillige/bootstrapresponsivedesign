About the University Writing Center

The UWC consultants are here to help you throughout the various stages of the writing process. We can help with:

  • brainstorming
  • outlining
  • drafting
  • revising

We are located on the first floor of the Student Success Center, Room Suite 1100-1167. Our hours of operation are:

  • Monday-Thursday: 10:00am-8:00pm
  • Friday: 10:00am-2:00pm
  • Sunday: 3:00pm-8:00pm

Staff Overview

Our Staff is made up of undergraduate, graduate, and faculty consultants. To work at the Writing Center, you can take the course on Writing Consultation. Additionally, you will participate in an apprecticeship with one of the more expereienced consultants to gain hands on experience.

Appointment Booking

Appointments are 30 or 60 minutes long either face-to-face or online. When booking your appointment, you can fill out the client report form to give your consultant an idea on what you all will be working on. You can book online today.
